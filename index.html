A bit about me


As described on LinkedIn:

Perhaps more than the business itself, what excites me most of all is working with a passionate, creative, and talented team. I am driven and inspired by the momentum of those I work with, and seek to drive and inspire them in return.

I am passionate about user experience and product development. These areas allow me to both find fulfillment in connecting with users, and challenge me to tackle new issues every day. I thrive in a fast-paced environment, working with all divisions within a company to help drive a unified vision and extraordinary success.

I value clear communication as the most important tool in the product manager's toolbox. I work hard to share ideas succinctly, whether in text, design, graphics, or video. As a team leader, I believe that colleagues (in particular developers and designers) must feel a sense of ownership and recognition in order to flourish.

With a deep respect for data, I consider all points of view carefully before making decisions, understanding that confident decisions are important signals both to team members and external constituents, such as users, press, and investors.

I look forward to the new opportunities, challenges, and partnerships that await me. Please do not hesitate to reach out (shaidavis@gmail.com) to tell me about your passions. I look forward to discussing them further.
